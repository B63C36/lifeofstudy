Tips & Tricks

First of all, above all, your health is your wealth. You Will NOT be able to study effectively if your mind and body is not at peace. Get the right sleep and eat the right foods, as humans we need energy to heal ourselves. Put yourself before your studies.

When dealing with multiple tasks, you need to take it one step at a time. Break down each task and tackle them one by one. Organise yourself woith a structure. Work looks more achievable when it is not one massive snowball.

We can boost motivation in different ways. First of all you need to understand the purpose of your studies. Create a sense of urgency. Look at successful people and unsuccessful people, understand that one of them put the work in and one did not. Being competitive is natural, so seeing other people succeed will make you also want to succeed.

Did you know that theres a 'Best time to study'? Between 4AM - 6AM, brain function is at 100%. Between 6AM to 8PM, brain function is at 50% & during night time brain function is at 20%.
